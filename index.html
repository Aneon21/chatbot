Simple Chatbot

This is a simple example of a chatbot using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. It can be further expanded on with additional conditional statements.

Currently there are 4 commands that the bot can recognize:


hello: responds with a randomly selected greeting from and array

time: tells you the current local time in 12 hours format

date: tells you the day of the week, and the date

weather: uses two web APIs, one to fetches the city you live in based on your IP, the second uses the city to find the weather info
